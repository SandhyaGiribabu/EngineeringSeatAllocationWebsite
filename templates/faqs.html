TNEA or the Tamil Nadu Engineering Admission is a procedure conducted by Anna University for admission of UG students of Tamil Nadu. The students are selected based on the marks scored in their XII Standard qualifying examination conducted by HSC, CBSE, ISC or equivalent state boards

Candidates may enroll for TNEA 2024 using the link provided in the home page, or by accessing the given link: Registration Page.

Last date for Registration and Filling of Application online is 31.05.2024.

The Applicant Portal supports most popular web browsers. However, if you are using Internet Explorer please ensure that you are using version 9 or above. Older versions of Internet Explorer are not supported. Alternatively, please try using either Mozilla Firefox or Google Chrome.
